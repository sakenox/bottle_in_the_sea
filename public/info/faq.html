Frequently Asked Questions

What is SeaNotes?

SeaNotes is a platform where users can create and discover hidden messages in a bottle. Share your thoughts anonymously and explore messages from others.

How do I create a message?

Click on the "Create a Bottle" button on the homepage and write your message. Once you're done, your message will be hidden for others to find.

How do I open a message?

Click on the "Open a Bottle" button on the homepage to discover a hidden message.

Can I delete a message I've created?

Currently, we do not offer the ability to delete messages once they have been created. Please make sure you are comfortable with the content you are sharing.

How can I report inappropriate content?

If you come across a message that violates our guidelines, you can report it by clicking on the "Report" button associated with the message. You can also email us at privacy@seanotes.se if you want faster action time.
