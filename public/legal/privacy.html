Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2nd July, 2024

At SeaNotes, accessible from seanotes.se one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that are collected and recorded by SeaNotes and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Message Content: The content of the messages created by users.
  • IP Address: The IP address from which messages are created.
  • Timestamp: The time at which messages are created and opened.
  • Report Count: The number of reports a message has received.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including:

  • To maintain, operate, and provide features and functionalities of the site.
  • To monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve user experience.
  • To detect and prevent abuse, fraud, and other harmful activity.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and the safety of our users.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Ads on our site. Google may collect information about users for advertising purposes. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for more details.

User Rights

Users have the right to create messages unless one of their messages has been reported too many times. Users may appeal to be unbanned by emailing appeal@seanotes.se.

Data Retention

Messages are periodically deleted from our servers to ensure fresh content. The exact retention period may vary based on the volume of messages.

Prohibited Activities

Users are prohibited from spamming, leaking real information, and posting inappropriate content such as explicit material. Users are not allowed to abuse the API or conduct DDos attacks on the site. If you see any inappropriate content, please report it to privacy@seanotes.se.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@seanotes.se.
